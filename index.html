Financial services adoption in Mexico

At a worldwide level, economies have become conscious about the relevance of financial inclusion as an enabler of development. This awareness has been recognized by several international organizations like the OECD and the UN, who consider financial inclusion national strategies a key factor to eradicate poverty, end hunger, gender equality and economic empowerment of women, promote economic growth and jobs, promote industry innovation and infrastructure, among others. The 2018 National Survey of Financial Inclusion is based on a 14,500 household sample all over the country and focuses on the access to 4 financial services: Debit accounts, Credit accounts, Insurance policies, and Retirement accounts.

Source: Self elaboration with data of the INEGI: National Survey of Financial Inclusion 2018
