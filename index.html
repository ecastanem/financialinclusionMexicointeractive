Financial Inclusion in Mexico

At a worldwide level, economies have become conscious about the relevance of financial inclusion as an enabler of development. In Mexico, the 2018 National Survey of Financial Inclusion, based on a 14,500 household sample all over the country, is used as a tool to measure the access to 4 financial services: Debit accounts, Credit accounts, Insurance policies, and Retirement accounts.
